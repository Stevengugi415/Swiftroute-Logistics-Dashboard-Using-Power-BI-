# Swiftroute-Logistics-Dashboard-Using-Power-BI-
SwiftRoute Logistics Dashboard

SwiftRoute Logistics operates a multi-hub delivery network with drivers and vehicles handling high daily order volumes. Management required a centralized analytics solution to monitor performance, identify delays, optimize capacity, and improve customer satisfaction. I designed and built an interactive logistics dashboard using Power BI to transform raw operational data into actionable business insights. In February 2024, SwiftRoute Logistics saw strong performance improvements, with On-Time Delivery rising to 81.6% and CSAT reaching 86.5%, despite a slight 3.7% dip in total orders....<img width="896" height="530" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/79bd4ae7-1ed4-4a79-841b-1dde4462c8e6" />

In this Driver’s Overview dashboard, my focus is to ensure that the logistics manager gets a clear overview of the workforce’s performance for a given period. I begin with a summary of the total number of active drivers to give the manager a basic overview. Next, I analyze the relationship between a driver’s experience and their performance rating to determine whether there’s a direct link between the two factors. I also focus on the drivers who have the most delays to encourage a shift in focus from assumption to accountability.
